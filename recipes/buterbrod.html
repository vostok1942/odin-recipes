Buterbrod

Description

This is a very simple recipe common to countries in Eastern Europe. At its most basic, it is just a slice of bread with some butter spread on it. If you're feeling fancy, you can add a slice of boiled egg on top of the butter, and/or a slice of sausage. For the best experience, enjoy with a warm cup of black tea!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • Butter
  • 2 boiled eggs

Steps

  1. Cut each slice of bread in half
  2. Slice each egg in half, so that you have 4 egg halves
  3. Spread butter over each slice of bread
  4. Put 1 egg half on each slice of bread
  5. Enjoy!
