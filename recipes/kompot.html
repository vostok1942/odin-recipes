Kompot

Description

Another easy recipe, this time it's a delicious drink to enjoy with your food!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Sugar
  • A few apples
  • A few apricots
  • A few peaches
  • A few strawberries
  • A few cherries

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a sauce pan
  2. Slice the apples, apricots, and peaches into wedges
  3. Put the fruits into the boiling water
  4. Add 3-5 tablespoons of sugar, depending on how sweet you want the kompot
  5. When the mixture boils again, lower the heat and leave it to cook for another 5-8 minutes
  6. Remove from heat and leave to cool
