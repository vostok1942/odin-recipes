Blini

Description

This is a quick recipe that teaches you how to make Russian pancakes â€” blini. Blini are different to traditional American pancakes because they are much thinner and use a more liquid-like batter than American pancakes. Like the buterbrod recipe, blini on their own, although delicious, are very simple. You can experiment with many different fillings, such as sour cream, jam, sour cream with jam, or maybe even some smoked salmon or caviar, if you're feeling luxurious.

Ingredients

  • 3 raw eggs
  • 1-2 tablespoons of sugar
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • 30 grams of melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix eggs and sugar in a bowl
  2. Add cup of milk to the mixture
  3. Gradually mix in flour until there are no lumps
  4. Add the remaining milk, some salt, and melted butter
  5. Mix together to create a runny batter
  6. Add some of the batter to a heated frying pan, so that it creates a thin layer
  7. After 1 minute, flip the pancake on the other side
  8. Remove the pancake when both sides are golden-brown, but not burnt!
  9. Repeat from step 6 until you run out of batter
